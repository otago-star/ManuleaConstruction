Who We Are

Manulea Construction is a local, family-owned New Zealand business based in The Glen, Dunedin — near Carisbrook (the old Rugby Stadium) and Barry Dell Plumbing — founded and operated by Sione Laiva'a Manulea after over 20 years' experience in the construction industry across Dunedin, Otago, and New Zealand. Our goal is to build a reputation for reliability and exceptional workmanship while offering competitive pricing to local Dunedin and Otago homeowners. We bring Pacific Island building values to every job, with a strong focus on renovations for families.
